# SEO Audit Report: groundfloor.com

**Date:** February 20, 2026
**Benchmarked Against:** Fundrise.com
**Target Market:** United States
**Data Source:** Ahrefs

---

## Executive Summary

Groundfloor.com is a real estate crowdfunding and lending platform with strong brand recognition but very limited non-branded organic search visibility. Traffic has declined roughly 27% over the past 12 months (from approximately 11,200 to 8,200 monthly visits), and the keyword portfolio is overwhelmingly dominated by branded search terms.

The primary competitor benchmarked in this audit is Fundrise.com, which commands nearly 7x more organic traffic and has built a vastly larger content footprint targeting educational and commercial real estate investing keywords.

The good news: Groundfloor has a solid foundation -- Domain Rating of 49, 1,253 referring domains, and backlinks from Google, Apple, Wikipedia, Bloomberg, and Investopedia. The gap versus Fundrise is a content and SEO execution gap, not a fundamental authority problem. The competitive landscape in real estate crowdfunding content is achievable with a focused content strategy.
